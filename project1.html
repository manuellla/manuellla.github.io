Autonomous Robot

Spacially aware autonomous navigation

This is my current project, where I collaborate with a team of students, PhDs, and professors to develop the algorithms needed for autonomous robot navigation. Our goal is to integrate sensor data and GPS to enable the robot to follow a path, avoid obstacles, and move efficiently.

We use ROS to control everything and we develp ROS nodes mainly in C++. We use Gazebo for simulation and a actuall robot to deploy the algorithms and sensors here on the real world.
